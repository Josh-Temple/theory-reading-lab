## Primary texts

- **Author**: Ferdinand de Saussure
  - **Title**: *Course in General Linguistics*
  - **Type**: primary text / foundational linguistics
  - **Publication year**: 1916 (posthumous edition; verify edition details before formal citation)
  - **Theme(s)**: Structuralism; language; semiotics
  - **Why relevant**: Core anchor for sign, difference, and the priority of relations within a linguistic system.
  - **Notes / cautions**: Commonly cited as the foundational text for structural linguistics, but edition and translation details should be checked before producing formal references.
